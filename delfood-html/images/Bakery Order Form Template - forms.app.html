        Why use an online bakery order form?

        If you have a bakery shop and want to receive orders online, a bakery form is all you need. While creating an online order form, you can easily list your delicious baked goods, such as cakes, pies, and bread, so that people can order them anytime - anywhere. But this is not all. There are many reasons why you should create a bakery order form for your pastry shop:

        • Reach more people: With online orders, your bakery shop is in every home and office that has internet. This will allow you to take more bakery orders and possibly expand your business.
        • Save time: Whether you take a custom or standard cake order, it can be time-consuming. But an online bakery order form will collect all the necessary information for you. This way, you can save time and focus more on your tasty cakes and pastries.
        • Automatically collect payment: Online order forms also allow you to accept payments for pastry orders easily. And customers can pay via secure payment gateways, such as PayPal or Stripe. 

        How to create the perfect bakery order form?

        Although baked goods in the product basket can be very different from others, the layout for these forms is quite similar. But there are a few things that will make your form great and outstanding.

        • Have a matching design with your website: By presenting your goods in an elegant form, you will impress your customers and increase your sales. Fortunately, forms.app offers you a highly customizable bakery order form sample. That means you can design your form just the way you like it. For example, you can try adding a pastry background image or changing the color set to suit your website design.
        • Receive custom cake orders: More often than not, people want to have special cakes or pastries for a birthday or an occasion. So adding a field for additional notes can help you take custom orders. Furthermore, you can even add an upload field to see the cake model they want. 
        • Collect contact details: Sometimes, some of the crispy cookies can be out of stock; in that case, you need to contact the customers and inform them. Collecting contact details is also necessary if you provide delivery service for your customers.
        • Enable direct message option: People may want to contact you for many reasons. For example, they may want to give extra information about their order, learn about wholesale prices (if any), or cancel/adjust their order. Thanks to the direct message option, you can allow them to do any of these via WhatsApp.
        • Add various payment methods: forms.app’s bakery order form template includes a payment field with two enabled options. So, Stripe and Paypal are offered as default payment methods. But forms.app also allows you to add more options for payments. For instance, you can try adding a cash option for deliveries. This way, your customer will be able to pay at the door or at the store by using cash.


        How to edit your bakery order form?

        forms.app provides great order form templates for your bakery shop. After adding your products to the product basket field, you can use the form as it is or customize it to create a special online order form. The cake order form template comes with the necessary fields, such as contact information, delivery address, payment, and so forth. But if you need to add extra fields, you can easily do so on the edit page.

        After selecting the bakery order form template, you will first find a product basket. Change the existing photos with the photos of your yummy pastries, add their names & descriptions, and lastly, their prices. Now people will easily select the products they want and add them to the basket. When you review or adjust the remaining questions, your form is ready to collect orders.

        How to design your online bakery order form?

        A professional design will definitely affect people’s choices. With forms.app, you can customize your bakery order form in a matter of seconds. forms.app offers you free themes that you can easily choose for your form. While this will only take a second and allow you to have a wonderful design, you may also want to make detailed changes to your form. Luckily, forms.app provides powerful features for designing.

        You will find numerous options on the design menu, such as changing the color set, font, background, and more. By using these options, you can have a matching design with your brand logo or website. After finishing your design, hit the eye button and preview your form. This way, you will be able to see how it will look on other PCs and mobile devices.

        How to choose the best options for your bakery order form?

        forms.app offers countless options for your cake order form. While the bakery order form template comes with some necessary options enabled, you can change them as you want. For example, you can enable push notifications to get instant notifications for new orders. Further, sending the customers an automated email for their purchase can also be a good choice for your business. 

        After the form settings, you can change the sharing options and complete your form. forms.app offers many sharing options for your form. For example, you can post the form on your social media accounts, send your partners via email, print it as a QR code for your flyers, or embed it on your website. Feel free to try out all the options on the settings page and fine-tune your form!

        How to review and download the order records?

        With forms.app’s user-friendly results page, you will easily review your records and see the special analyses. This way, you will know how many orders you have received and how much profit you made in a specific time frame. You can also download and store the records on any device with a few clicks. With forms.app’s baker order form template, create your form easily and effortlessly!
